Watching List

I tend to watch a lot of random shows and movies. So best only if I add the biggest and/or the best.

  • Shogun: Best show I have watched. 5/5.
  • 3 Body Problem (Season 1): Some of the dialogues were badly written especially in the beginning. Wish it had more science, as in the book(s). But the show is good overall. Wish it had more nuanced character developments given the added character-focus in the show. 4/5.
  • Murder Mubarak: I don't remember. Probably not rated high.
  • The Gentlemen: Good acting. Nice comedy lines.
  • The Maze Runner Trilogy: Meh.
  • Spirited Away: I love Hayao Miyazaki.
  • The Boy and the Heron: 5/5.
  • Kung Fu Panda 4: I guess end of an era? Felt like a long youtube video.
  • Dune 2: Absolutely amazing. This is going to be bigger than Star Wars. 5/5.
